Are Geese Good or Evil?

An Educational Debate

Geese Are Good!

The hate on geese must stop! Geese are just normal birds that live their lives
based on instinct. They look very cute and their ducklings are very fluffy!
As long as you do not bother them, they will not bother you. Besides, we need
to be understanding that they have to migrate thousands of miles just to hibernate.
They just might be a little cranky.

Geese are Evil!

Geese are evil incarnate! They attack unprompted and are incredibly selfish.
Their poops have plagued Canadian parks for years and has only been getting worse
through the years. They lay their nests in inconvenient places and force traffic to
stop for them. Their protection laws must be changed and we should be allowed
to kill them.
